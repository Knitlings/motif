About

Motif: create patterns, see them repeat.

I built Motif because trying to design colourwork knitting patterns in full-featured design software like Photoshop or Inkscape was incredibly frustrating. Those are powerful tools do everything, while Motif aims to do one thing well: let you draw grid-based patterns and see how they look when they repeat.

Features

  • Live tiled preview: See customisable repeats
  • Flexible grid: Create grids from 2×2 to 100×100 cells
  • Custom aspect ratios: Square cells or custom dimensions
  • Multi-colour support: Work with up to 20 colours per pattern
  • Export options: Save your work as PNG, SVG, or JSON

Technical details

Motif uses vanilla JavaScript and runs entirely in your browser. Your patterns are saved locally, meaning nothing leaves your device. It's free, open source (MIT license), and available on GitHub.

Privacy & Data Storage

Motif stores all your work locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your patterns are automatically saved as you work
  • Your work persists between sessions on the same browser and device
  • Clearing your browser data will delete your saved work

I don't collect or store any of your pattern data. Motif is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how the site is used. Cloudflare Web Analytics is privacy-first and doesn't use cookies or track individual users. For more details, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
